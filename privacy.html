Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

When you use SovitX Digital, we may collect the following information:

  • Name and contact details provided during purchase
  • UPI transaction reference IDs for order verification
  • Device information and browser type for analytics
  • Pages visited and interactions on our website

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To provide customer support
  • To send important product updates (not spam)
  • To improve our website and product offerings
  • To prevent fraud and maintain platform security

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party. Your data is only used internally to serve you better.

We use Firebase (Google) for our database. Data is stored securely on Google's servers and governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Cookies

We use localStorage to store your cart and wishlist preferences locally in your browser. No tracking cookies are used. You can clear this data anytime through your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

For privacy-related queries, contact us at support@sovitx.in or via Telegram @sovitxdigital.
